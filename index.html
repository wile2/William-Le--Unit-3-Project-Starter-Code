Issac's Adventure

You are having a slumber under an apple tree. Suddenly, you awake from an fallen apple hitting your thick skull. You grab the apple with your hand and grasp it gently, what do you do with it?

You take a scrumptious bite out of the apple, it tastes sweet and replenishing. Double click the apple to take bites out of the apple!

You start to think about falling down a cliff. Your brain expands, little by little. You stand up and drop the apple for a second time, catching it with your other hand. Double click the brain to expand your knowledge more!

The flesh of the apple starts to brown from oxidation, so you decide to throw it away and stand up.

After you stand back up, you take a stroll back home.

You have broken the limits of cognitive human intelligence. Standing up, your thinking starts to lean heavily on physics and algebra.

After you've stood for 5 minutes, you take a stroll back home, taking the apple with you.
